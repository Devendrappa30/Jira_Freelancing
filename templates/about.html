My Story

10 Years of Making Teams Ship Faster

I started as a Business Analyst in 2015 at Estuate Software, where I first got my hands on Zendesk CRM implementations. I discovered something that would define my career: the right tool, configured correctly, changes everything for a team.

Over the next decade — through roles at Estuate, Tesser Insights, and Kloudserve — I led implementations across Zendesk, Microsoft Dynamics CRM/ERP, and Salesforce. I managed sprint teams, ran Scrum ceremonies, built dashboards for C-level stakeholders, and helped organizations shift from spreadsheet chaos to structured, measurable delivery.

Today, I focus entirely on what I do best: Jira implementation and Agile coaching. As a freelancer, I bring enterprise-grade expertise to teams of every size — startups to MNCs — without the overhead of a large consultancy.

I earned my Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) from Scrum Alliance, completed Google's Project Management certificate, and stay current through PMI's Generative AI program. I believe Jira is only as good as the thinking behind its setup — and that's where I add the most value.
